Why Join?


We are a new server with the goal of providing an all in one experience for all Minecraft players!
We grew from a single survival world to a network with survival, skyblock, towny and skygrid if you are up for an extreme parkour survival test.
Read more about the different game modes below!



Survival

A multiplayer survival server with a chill community.
It has an economy, grief protection, easy teleportation so you can visit your friends if they want you to and more!





Towny

Build a town with your friends and allow people all over the world to join!
Form an alliance or play it neutral, it doesn't matter!
Will you be the richest city, or will you be unable to afford the daily bills?
It has an economy, alliances, grief protection (only town members can access your plot) and a whole load more!



Skyblock


Form and build an island in midair, safe from death by void.
Pick from one of 3 starter templates: mushroom, desert and jungle.
Can you survive with the starter resources, or will you burn in your cobblestone generator?
It has a leveling system, an economy, no pvp, grief protection outsiders can't touch your island and a ton more cool stuff!



Skygrid


The ultimate challenge! You are in midair surrounded by blocks with a gap between them!
Will you be able to survive and get OP gear or will you plummet to your death too quickly?
It has the ability to create a team island, easy teleportation, a challenging aspect, grief proof islands and a lot more fun stuff!
