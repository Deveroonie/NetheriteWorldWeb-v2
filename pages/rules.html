Netherite World Rules!



















Network wide rules!
  1. No swearing.
  2. No scamming.
  3. Treat everyone with respect.
  4. English only in chat, please keep other languages in PMs.
  5. Staff have the final say.
  6. Keep your username and skin appropriate.
  7. No cheating/using cheat clients.
  8. No griefing other players builds.
  9. Do not cause lag to the server intentially. This includes lag machines, dropping thousands of items and more.
  10. Do not use bugs or exploits in the game to duplicate items or anything else involving exploits/bugs.
  11. Do not leak other players personal information without their consent.
  12. Advertising other servers or anything else is prohibited.
  13. You are not allowed to trade server items for real life (IRL) money.
  14. Do not threaten to DDoS/DoX/SWAT anyone on the server.
  15. Do not send spoilers to movies or anything else.
  16. Bypassing the chat filter is prohibited.
  17. Saying something to confuse other players is not allowed.
  18. You are not allowed to use another account to bypass a ban/mute.
  19. Be honest with staff. If you are caught lying to staff you will be banned.

Discord Rules!
  1. You must be at least 13 years old to participate in this server.
    • You must follow Discord's Terms Of Service
    • You must follow Discord's Community Guidelines
  2. You are not allowed to post NSFW content.
  3. You are not allowed to swear in chat.
  4. You must be respectful to other server members at all times.
